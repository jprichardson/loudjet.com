Negativity, Fear, and the Lizard Brain

Negativity, Fear, and the Lizard Brain

If you catch yourself saying words like…

“We can’t compete with…”

“It’ll never happen!”

“I’m afraid that…”

These statements are based on negativity and fear. They are poisonous thoughts. You can compete. Competition validates the market. If Mark Zuckerberg was worried about competing with MySpace, we wouldn’t have Facebook. If Steve Jobs accepted the carrier-dominate mobile phone industry, we wouldn’t have the iPhone.

You must quiet the lizard brain.
