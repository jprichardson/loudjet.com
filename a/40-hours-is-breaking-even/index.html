40 Hours Is Breaking Even

40 Hours Is Breaking Even

I read an article yesterday that made a great point. Unfortunately, I don’t remember the link. But the point was that to get ahead in life you must put in more than 40 hours. I know, it sounds so simple, but yet so many people don’t think this way.

Whatever your career is, you must put in more than 40 hours. Maybe you want a promotion? Maybe you want to build a company? Just remember, 40 hours is breaking even.
